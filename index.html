Emirates
Nutrition
Association

Emirates Nutrition Association is responsible for:


Develop the scientific and professional performance of the members and achieve the scientific communication between them.

Facilitate the exchange of scientific outcomes and ideas in the Association's field of interest between the concerned bodies and institutions nationally and internationally.

Enhance the scientific application of nutrition and dietetics and the related subjects.

Strengthening the training and learning in the field of food science, nutrition, and dietetics.

Provide scientific advice in the field of the Association.

Addressing the challenges and the growing negative impact of malnutrition on public health.

Advance the levels of health and nutrition awareness among the public.

Contribute to the improvement of the criteria and standards of practice within the profession.
